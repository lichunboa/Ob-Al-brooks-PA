# ROSE: 2+2+2 setup [Failure of Failure is Swing]

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DDMzUSh1jKE 

- TTR two DB/DT + two consecutive bar setup 【双顶/底+双bar】
    - **Setup**: two sets of DB and DT in TTR
    - **Entry**: 2 consecutive bar **closes beyond 50% of TR**
        - To long: 1 tick about the 2nd bull bar
        - To short: 1 tick below the 2nd bear bar
    - **Target**: scalping for 0.5R or 1R and leave a runner for 2R
    - **Stop**:  1 tick below the 1st bull bar or above the 1st bear bar
        - 如果第二个bar的open被反方向吞没了，提前离场
