- Trend bars and TR bars
    - Trend bars and TR bars
        
        
        | **trend bar** | **TR bars** |
        | --- | --- |
        | close near top or bottom | close at middle |
        | small tail | big tail |
        | big body | small body |
        | small overlap with prior bars | overlap with prior bars |
        | form a trend | trend bars quickly reverse |
